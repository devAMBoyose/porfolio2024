Video Ads

Audio Visual Presentation

"20 Years of Excellence: A Tribute to Biosite Medical Instruments"

I created this audio-visual presentation specifically for the 20th Anniversary Convention of Biosite Medical Instruments, to honor and celebrate their two decades of groundbreaking work in the medical technology field. The video combines visual storytelling, music, and narrative to reflect the journey, values, and legacy of Biosite—from their early beginnings to their current status as a trusted leader in diagnostic innovation. Every scene and sound was carefully chosen to highlight not just their technological milestones, but also the human stories behind them—the doctors, patients, and professionals whose lives have been positively impacted by Biosite's contributions to healthcare. My goal in creating this piece was to craft more than just a timeline—I wanted it to evoke emotion, pride, and reflection, while serving as a memorable tribute during this significant milestone. The background music was added to enhance the emotional tone of the video, allowing it to resonate deeply with viewers during the live convention presentation. It was a privilege to develop this project in collaboration with the Biosite team, and I hope it stands as a meaningful thank-you to everyone who has been part of their incredible 20-year journey.
